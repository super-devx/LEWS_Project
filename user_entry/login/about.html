Project Overview

Comprehensive landslide monitoring and early warning system

Our Vision

To create a nationwide network of landslide early warning systems that protect communities and infrastructure through advanced technology and real-time monitoring.

Our Mission

To develop, deploy, and maintain cutting-edge sensor technology integrated with predictive analytics to provide timely warnings and save lives in landslide-prone areas.

Our Values

Innovation, accuracy, reliability, and community safety drive everything we do. We are committed to excellence in research, development, and deployment.

A premier research institution under the Council of Scientific and Industrial Research (CSIR), bringing decades of expertise in construction engineering, geotechnical studies, and disaster mitigation technologies.

  • Structural Analysis

IIT-Roorkee

Indian Institute of Technology

One of India's foremost engineering and research institutions, contributing advanced computational modeling, sensor technology, and cutting-edge research in earth sciences and civil engineering.

  • Computational Modeling
  • Sensor Technology
  • Data Analytics
  • Earth Sciences
Funded By

National Mission on Himalayan Studies (NMHS)

This project is proudly funded by NMHS, a flagship program of the Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change, Government of India.

NMHS aims to evolve a sustainable national capacity to understand the complexities of Himalayan ecosystems and their responses to climate change. Our landslide early warning system aligns perfectly with this mission by protecting communities in the Himalayan region.

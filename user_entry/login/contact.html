Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

You need to register for an account and wait for admin approval. Once approved, you can login and access real-time sensor data and analytics.

LEWS monitors various sensors including piezometers, rain gauges, tiltmeters, crack meters, and other geotechnical instruments across multiple NMHS sites.

After logging in, navigate to the "Request Dataset" section where you can select date ranges and sensor types to download data in CSV format.

For technical issues, email support@lews.gov.in or call our support line during business hours. For emergencies, use the 24/7 hotline.

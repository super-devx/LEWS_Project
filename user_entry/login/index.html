About LEWS

Joint Collaborative Development for Himalayan Safety

The Landslide Early Warning System (LEWS) is a state-of-the-art monitoring platform designed to protect vulnerable communities in landslide-prone regions of the Himalayas.

This system has been developed through a collaborative effort between two premier Indian research institutions, combining expertise in structural engineering, geotechnical monitoring, and advanced data analytics.

Our Mission

To provide accurate, timely, and actionable early warnings for landslide hazards, enabling communities and authorities to take preventive measures and save lives through the power of continuous monitoring and advanced sensor technology.

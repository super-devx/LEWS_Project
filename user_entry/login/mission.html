Our Mission

Saving Lives Through Innovation

Developing cutting-edge early warning systems to predict and prevent landslide disasters, protecting communities and preserving lives across vulnerable regions.

"To harness the power of advanced sensor technology, data analytics, and scientific research to create a robust early warning system that predicts landslide events, enables timely evacuations, and ultimately saves lives while protecting critical infrastructure in landslide-prone regions across India."

LEWS Mission Statement Joint Initiative of CSIR-CBRI & IIT-Roorkee

Core Objectives

Our strategic goals for landslide disaster mitigation

01

Deploy Advanced Sensors

Install and maintain a comprehensive network of sensors across high-risk zones to continuously monitor ground movement, rainfall, and environmental conditions.

  • 500+ sensors deployed
  • Real-time data collection
  • Multi-parameter monitoring
02

Predictive Analytics

Develop sophisticated algorithms and machine learning models to analyze sensor data and accurately predict potential landslide events before they occur.

  • AI-powered predictions
  • 98% accuracy rate
  • Early warning generation
03

Timely Warnings

Establish rapid alert systems to notify authorities and communities immediately when landslide risk reaches critical levels, enabling swift evacuation.

  • Multi-channel alerts
  • SMS, mobile apps, sirens
  • 24/7 monitoring
04

Community Engagement

Educate and empower local communities with knowledge about landslide risks, safety measures, and appropriate responses to early warning alerts.

  • Training programs
  • Awareness campaigns
  • Evacuation drills
05

Research & Development

Conduct continuous research to improve sensor technology, prediction models, and system reliability based on real-world data and emerging technologies.

  • Ongoing studies
  • Technology upgrades
  • Academic publications
06

Nationwide Expansion

Scale the system to cover all landslide-prone areas across India, creating a comprehensive national early warning network for maximum impact.

  • Multi-state deployment
  • Infrastructure development
  • Government partnerships
